PitchFX Search

Research, Report, Investigate

We allow you to search through any player's pitches and at-bats with quick facets on things like batted ball angles, pitch speed, pitch types, hit type, and many others.

How

Using the atbat-mongodb project, we pull pitch and at-bat data from each day's baseball games and store it in our own MongoDB database. This site differs from other PitchFX search sites in that it front-loads most of the data for a player into your browser and lets your browser do the work. This makes facetting much, much quicker.

Data from each day will be available by 6AM EST on the following day.

Problems? If you find any issues with this site you can report them through the GitHub project.
